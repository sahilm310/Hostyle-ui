WCAG - Web Content Accessibility Guidelines

4.5:1  Contrast Ratio

Make sure colours in the foreground have a high enough contrast against the background, for users who are visually impaired.

Care for the Colour Blind

Users may not be able to distinguish between different shades of a colour. Always check your colour palette for all types of colour blindness.
